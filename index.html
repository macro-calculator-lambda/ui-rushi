About Our Calorie and Macro Calculator

Optimizing the calories and foods that go into your body is the most important thing you can do in order to reach your fitness goals. Our calculator is an advanced algorithm that computes your macro needs, based on your specific personal information and lifestyle habits

Lose, Maintain, or Gain?

  • Lose puts you in a calorie deficit which promotes safe, steady weight loss
  • Maintain allows you to eat at macro levels that will keep you at current weight
  • Gain puts you in a calorie surplus that is designed for people who are wanting to build muscle fast in conjunction with a comprehensive weight training program
